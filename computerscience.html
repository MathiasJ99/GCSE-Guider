Learning

Online videos

The main way to learn online is for someone to go through and explain what they are doing and why. As a result, this is why I use YouTube videos as I find them very useful.

Youtube channels/ Learning videos I recommend are:

  • Computer Science Tutor

    Seneca is a relatively new site which can be used for help with sciences and other subjects. In my class people either loved or hated their style of teaching/practicing.
