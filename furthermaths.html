Further Maths

Extension to normal maths by combining the hardest parts of GCSE maths and the first year of A-level Maths (AS). By doing Further maths it will give you an edge when it comes to your first year of A level as it is mainly recapped.

Learning

There is one YouTube channel which I used to learn topics which we did not learn about in class this isCorbett maths I found this extremely useful and was the only site I could find.

Further Maths is not taken by many people for GCSE and as a result, there are not a lot of resources available online. Consequently, if you can learn from them, revision guides can be very helpful and the one I would is: .

9-4 AQA Level 2 Certificate: Further Maths - Revision Guide

Practicing

Topic questions

As mentioned above the only resource questions I could find were from corbett maths . These are still good as they have a wide range of varying difficulties and answers.
