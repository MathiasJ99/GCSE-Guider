Biology

Required for Biology A-level which is needed if you want to have a career in the medical industry.

Learning

Online videos

The main way to learn online is for someone to go through and explain what they are doing and why. As a result, this is why I use YouTube videos as I find them very useful.

Youtube channels/ Learning videos I recommend are:

  • My GCSE Science (paid)
  • free science lessons
  • Fuse School(not specifially GCSE)

    • The channels/sites listed above have loads of content covering each subtopic on the science specification. All of these sources in my opinion are very informative and valuable however they each have there own style of teaching; My GCSE Science prefers longer videos whereas Fuse School and free science lessons prefer shorter videos.

    Online videos

    Seneca is a relatively new site which can be used for help with sciences and other subjects. In my class people either loved or hated their style of teaching/practicing.

    Memorization

    Biology has lots of key information that students are recommended to know. Consequently, I recommend flashcards or Anki and using SRL . This can be used to gain easy marks on low-level questions.

    Revision Guides

    Some people find these helpful as they condense notes and especially for biology, can explain diagrams. These are not for everyone.

    AQA ONLY!!!

    Sciences Tips

    Learn required practicals. At least one will come up in some form and it can be an easy 6 marks or more.

    Practicing

    Topic questions

    Seneca can be used for topic questions as it has the whole specification. However, it can be a bit annoying to skip through the dialogue.

    Similarly Physics and Maths tutor has most of the topic questions for Biology GCSE which can be downloaded.

    Daily questions

    N/A.

    Past Papers

    When done in time conditions and marked they are the best indicator of grades. The downside is that not everyone has 2 hours to complete and mark them.

    workbooks

    workbooks are another resource which is great as they provide questions on each topic of the specification. These can be used instead of topic questions Once you have identified what you need to work on.
