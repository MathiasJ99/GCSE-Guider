Maths

Maths is the subject with possibly the most uses and is required for a range of A-level options. Consequently having a strong foundation in maths will help and will probably be required for a range of subjects.

Learning

The main way to learn online is for someone to go through and explain what they are doing and why. As a result, this is why I use YouTube videos as I find them very useful.

Youtube channels I recommend are:

  • maths genie
  • fuse school
  • corbett maths

    • These YouTube channels have playlists that cover practically everything in the spec so you can focus on areas that you are struggling with or do not know.

    Practicing

    Topic questions

    Once you have an understanding of the topic you can use topic questions to make sure that you have a solid understanding. For me, I used maths genieas that was what my teachers recommended.

    Daily questions

    For little daily questions, I would recommend doing acorbett maths five a dayas they test a wide range of topics and they have one for each grade. These are also good for identifying the areas you are weak at if you do not have time for past papers.

    workbooks

    workbooks are another resource which is great as they provide questions on each topic of the specification. These can be used instead of topic questions.

    Edexcel Only

    If you prefer workbooks the ones I would recommend the Gcse Edexecel Maths as it covers the whole 9-1 spec.

    Edexcel Only

    This boook is for if you are aiming for 8's and 9's as it is a booster workbook which only has grade 8/9 questions.
